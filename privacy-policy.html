Privacy Policy

Salah Reminder  •  Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Salah Reminder mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. We built Salah Reminder to work fully offline and to respect your privacy — please read below for the full details.

1. Summary

  • Salah Reminder does not require an account or sign-up.
  • Salah Reminder does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server.
  • All prayer times, reminder settings, and prayer-tracking records are stored locally on your device only.
  • The App does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • The App works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

2. Information We Access (On-Device Only)

To provide its core features, the App may access the following information. This data is processed only on your device and is never sent to us or to any third party.

2.1 Location

The App may access your device's approximate or precise location (depending on the permission you grant) solely to calculate accurate prayer (Salah) timings for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha based on where you are. If you do not grant location permission, you can still manually set your city or manually enter prayer times.

Location data is used only for the on-device calculation and is not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

2.2 Notifications

The App uses your device's notification permission to send you prayer time reminders and azaan alerts. Notification scheduling happens entirely on your device.

2.3 Prayer Tracking Data

When you mark a prayer as offered, missed, or made up, this record is saved in local storage on your device. This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is never shared with us or any third party
  • Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App or clear its data

2.4 Device Storage

The App may request storage permission (where applicable) to save your settings and prayer records locally on your device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your precise identity or account information (no account exists)
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data transmitted to external servers, since the App does not connect to the internet to function

4. Third-Party Services

Salah Reminder does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The App does not share any data with third parties because no data leaves your device.

If this changes in a future update (for example, if we introduce optional cloud backup, crash reporting, or similar features), this Privacy Policy will be updated in advance, and where required, we will ask for your consent before enabling such features.

5. Children's Privacy

Salah Reminder does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the App does not collect personal data at all, it can be used safely by users of all ages.

6. Data Security

Because all data stays on your device and is never transmitted, the security of your prayer records and settings depends on your device's own security (e.g., screen lock, device encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

7. Your Choices and Control

  • Location permission: You can deny or revoke location permission at any time from your device settings. The App will continue to work using manually entered prayer times.
  • Notification permission: You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.
  • Deleting your data: Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App's storage/cache from your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App's functionality or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
